# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 2, 2025

ConvertFix ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our Service at convertfix.app.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
When you purchase an audit, we collect:
- **Email address:** To deliver your audit report
- **Landing page URL:** The webpage you want us to analyze
- **Payment information:** Processed and stored by Paddle (our payment processor) - we never see your credit card details

### 1.2 Information We Automatically Collect
When you visit our website, we may collect:
- **Usage data:** Pages viewed, time spent, clicks
- **Device information:** Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Cookies:** Small files stored on your device (see Cookies section below)

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Paddle:** Confirms payment success and provides transaction details (not your full payment card info)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### 2.1 Provide the Service
- Generate your AI-powered audit report
- Deliver the PDF report via email
- Process your payment through Paddle
- Provide customer support

### 2.2 Improve the Service
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI models
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Develop new features based on user needs

### 2.3 Communications
- Send transactional emails (order confirmation, delivery)
- Respond to your inquiries
- Send service updates (only if material changes occur)

**We will NEVER:**
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Send you marketing emails (unless you explicitly opt in)
- Share your data with advertisers

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 Service Providers
We share information with trusted service providers who help us operate:

- **Paddle.com:** Payment processing and tax compliance (Merchant of Record)
- **Resend:** Email delivery service for sending audit reports
- **Anthropic (Claude API):** AI analysis of landing pages
- **Vercel:** Website hosting and infrastructure

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for providing services to us.

### 3.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes (court orders, subpoenas)
- Protect our rights, property, or safety
- Prevent fraud or abuse of the Service

### 3.3 Business Transfers
If ConvertFix is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new owners. You will be notified of any such change.

## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 Audit Data
- **Landing page URL and content:** Stored for 30 days, then automatically deleted
- **Generated audit reports:** Stored for 30 days, then automatically deleted
- **Email address:** Retained for 90 days for customer support, then deleted

### 4.2 Payment Data
- **Transaction records:** Retained by Paddle for tax and legal compliance (typically 7 years)
- **We do not store payment card information** - this is handled exclusively by Paddle

### 4.3 Account Deletion
Since we don't have user accounts, there's no account to delete. Your data automatically expires per the retention schedule above.

To request immediate deletion, email us at support@convertfix.app.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

### 6.1 What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website.

### 6.2 Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies (Required):**
- Session cookies: Keep you logged in during checkout
- Security cookies: Prevent fraud and abuse

**Analytics Cookies (Optional):**
- Google Analytics: Track website usage and traffic sources
- These can be disabled in your browser settings

**We do NOT use:**
- Advertising cookies
- Third-party tracking pixels (except Paddle for payment processing)

### 6.3 Managing Cookies
You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features.

We take security seriously and implement industry-standard measures:

### 7.1 Technical Safeguards
